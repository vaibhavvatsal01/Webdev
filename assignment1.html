Welcome to ABES

ABES Engineering College in Ghaziabad is a college offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs, established over two decades ago under the Society for Educational Excellence. It focuses on holistic student development, academic excellence, and innovation, with courses including B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, and MBA. The college boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, strong placement records with high packages, and a commitment to industry-relevant skills through programs like robotics and AI, supported by a dedicated faculty and research initiatives.
